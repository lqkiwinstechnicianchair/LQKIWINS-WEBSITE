JULIA DO

ART HISTORIAN

A historian is in charge of two categories: photography and arts/crafts. For photography, historians must strive to go to every volunteering event to capture countless photos of members volunteering. Therefore, they must provide themselves a high-quality camera and upload the photos on a photo-sharing website such as Facebook. For arts/crafts, historians are responsible for making physical art creations whichever the club demands such as posters and scrapbook. He or she must not run out of creative ideas to keep the club an elegant reputation.

Each November, KIWIN'S hosts the annual Fall Rally! Fall Rally is a day dedicated to raising awareness of and supporting the Pediatric Trauma Program. The Pediatric Trauma Program, commonly known as PTP, is one of our three district projects. This is a day where all nine divisions of CNH KIWIN'S unite to not only focus on raising awareness for PTP, but to show what each division is made of through spirit battles! This year, Fall Rally North will be on November 12th at Lincoln Park while South will be on November 19th at William Peak Park! So make sure you’re there to not only help support and bring light on PTP, but also your division as each division fights for the spirit stick!
